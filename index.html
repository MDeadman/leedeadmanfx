ABOUT

I am a special effects artist based in Toronto, ON. I graduated with honors from Sheridan College's Advanced Special Effects Makeup, Prosthetics and Props program in 2016, after graduating from the college's Visual and Creative Arts program in 2015. Born in Tucson, Arizona I am a dual citizen and am legally eligible to work in both Canada and the United States.

I first discovered my passion for bringing characters to life through costume, makeup and props in 2010 when I was introduced to cosplay, a form of costuming where people dress as characters from popular culture. I love prolem solving and coming up with ways to take 2D characters and designs and bring them to life in 3D and so I strive to constantly keep learning new skills and techniques and to improve my costuming and sfx skills.
